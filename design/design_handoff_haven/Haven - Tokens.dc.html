RULE At most three accents per screen; most use one or two. The whole system reads ~95% black-and-white. Text, icons and lines are always ink — colour appears only as a fill. Colour is muted on Kaleido: a vivid hue renders as a soft pastel, so we design at the muted intent shown above.

Data visualisation

INK LINE-ART · NO GRADIENTS · 300 PPI

Charts are 2px ink lines on white. Colour appears only as a flat band fill behind the data: sage for a healthy/comfort range, rust for an active/alert state. Axes and values are IBM Plex Mono. Grid is hairline; the “now” point is a filled dot, a threshold crossing a hollow ring.
